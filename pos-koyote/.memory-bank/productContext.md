# Product Context

## Users
- Cashiers running rapid sales at a physical counter.
- Managers reviewing daily activity and inventory health.

## Workflows
- Start a new sale and scan or search for products.
- Manage inventory counts for singles, sealed product, and accessories.
- Update pricing and product metadata.

## UX Expectations
- Dark theme to reduce glare.
- Large targets for touch and scanner workflows.
- Minimal latency, even without network access.
